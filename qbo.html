QBO INNOVATION HUB

QBO is a Filipino innovation hub with the aim to aid startups and move ideas forward. The Agency is tasked to create the logo, identity and branding materials. This abstract interpretation of the bahay-kubo shows the modern nature of QBO’s business, and how it’s gone beyond the one-room house to a greater world of networking.
