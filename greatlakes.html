Great Lakes Seaway Partnership Miscellaneous Graphics

The team at Sway was tasked to create the website for the Great Lakes Seaway Partnership. Part of the assets needed were animated images that state fast facts. Some pages of the site, which show data from different states, also needed to be translated into downloadable PDFs. Another task was to set up the social media accounts of the Great Lakes to reflect their new brand and identity. Header images that span across Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Youtube and Google+ were created.
