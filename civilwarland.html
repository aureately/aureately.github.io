CIVILWARLAND IN BAD DECLINE

This speculative redesign of George Saunders’s short story collection reflects the theme of a post-apocalyptic America, now overrun by consumerist culture and adults who act without regard for the younger generation. Saunders delivers this narrative with dark wit; this voice is carried over to the cover’s playful yet sinister imagery.
