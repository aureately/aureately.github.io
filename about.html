Hello! My name is Trisha and I create cool things.

I grew up in Manila, Philippines, with two very techie parents who thought it was a good idea to introduce Flash, the Corel Suite, and FrontPage as playthings to an 11-year-old. Little did they know that these childhood hobbies, tempered through teenage years of creating games and websites, would prompt that young one to fly to the United States and pursue a B.F.A. in Graphic Design.

The program at Old Dominion University provided me a good background in print design, and various side projects, hackathons and internships have allowed me to explore the digital realm. I find it fascinating to push an idea through various platforms, and more so, create things that tell a story and make a difference.
