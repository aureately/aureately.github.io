Hello!

My name is Trisha and I make nice things.

I grew up in Manila, Philippines, with two very techie parents who thought it was a good idea to introduce Flash, the Corel Suite, and FrontPage as playthings to an 11-year-old. Little did they know that these childhood hobbies, tempered through teenage years of creating games and websites for other people just for fun of it, would actually convince that kid that design is The One.

I am currently a designer living in Norfolk, Virginia, always trying to learn a bunch of stuff, and with a ton of projects in tow.
