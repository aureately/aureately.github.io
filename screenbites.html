M&M'S SCREENBITE AWARDS 2017

M&M’S wants to position itself as the go-to screentime snack. To introduce this, BBDO Guerrero created an online campaign inviting people to submit their reactions to video content while snacking on M&M’S.

We developed a microsite to accept entries and display the reactions for voting, as well as created bumper ads and social posts to drive people to join.

This regional competition ran across the Philippines, Malaysia and Thailand. We worked with BBDO Malaysia and BBDO Thailand to ensure optimal experience across its user base and to make sure that the campaign translates well across the different languages.
