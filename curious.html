Curious

Poster

The discovery of a dead poodle in the middle of the night led Christopher, a young boy with Aspergers, to confront the truth that his father has kept from him all his life. In this poster, excerpts from the letters Christopher finds create the imagery of the dog, showing the parallel between this present act of rage to the past events that led up to it.
