SentaraNEWS Website

The team at Sway was challenged to redesign the SentaraNEWS website to make it a better experience for its audience, mainly those who work in media.

In the previous version, the featured article overwhelmed the entire page; the goal for this design revamp is to tone that down, and make sure that the other stories following the feature are of equal weight and not lost beneath the fold.

Each story has a more accessible means for the users to directly contact Sentara representatives if they want to request for more information. A tagging system is set in place, filtering the articles and allowing the users to subscribe to related topics.
