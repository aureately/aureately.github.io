THE WRIGHT WEDDING

It was a huge honor to be given the opportunity to design for the wedding of two awesome friends. They envisioned their day to be all-crafted — from the bouquets and table setting, to the thousand paper cranes scattered across the venue. The challenge was for the invitations to embody their creativity which is one of the strengths of their relationship.

We have the Save-the-Date which the receiver could cut down into a paper crane ornament, the mini detail cards, and the tri-fold invitation as a subtle symbol for their love of paper crafts.
